terra schneider

terra schneider is a creative technologist based in detroit, mi. their work focuses on bio-inspired computing, biology and physics models, economics, and social refuse.
they are currently a student at the university of michigan after a decade-long hiatus from school spent working, traveling, and self-educating. terra is pursuing a b.s. in computer science, a b.s. in anthropology, and a minor in sts studies.
